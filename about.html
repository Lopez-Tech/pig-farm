About Us

Family Pig Farm is a family-owned farm based in Lagos. We believe in clean, ethical farming and raising healthy pigs for both families and businesses. Our mission is to provide quality livestock and exceptional customer service.

Founded in 2020, we have been committed to sustainable farming practices and ensuring that every pig receives proper care and nutrition.

Our team treats every customer like family and ensures you get the best products with honesty and reliability.
